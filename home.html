Why Mechanical Keyboard

Have you counted how many hours you spend on the computer and how much of your life revolves around it? The answer most likely will be, a lot. So we might as well make those hours as pleasant as possible. That being said, why not invest in something you use very often?

Mechanical keyboards are mood-impacting both from a productivity and pleasure standpoint. The way it is satisfying to your touch could help you type for longer periods of time or make gaming more enjoyable for you to improve your performance.

Productive and gaming at the same time, why not?

Wise men said that two is better than one. But that is not the case for everything, including your gears. When you find "the one" that could hit the sweet spot between aesthetically-pleasing and highly-functional mechanical keyboard both for your productivity and your gaming experience, you'll see how one could actually do so much. Again, if you find the right one.

NOIR is tailored to help you perform and feel at your best both for working and gaming hours because you don't have to choose between quality, function and looks. You can have them all packed in a single mechanical keyboard.
